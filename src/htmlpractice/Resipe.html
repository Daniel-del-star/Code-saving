My favorite Recipe: Jollof Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Oil
  • Onions
  • Pepper

Steps to prepare

  1. Blend the tomatoes, red bell pepper, ½ red onion, scotch bonnets, garlic, and ginger into a smooth purée. Simmer the purée over medium heat for about 15 minutes, stirring, until slightly reduced and thickened.
  2. Sauté the aromatics
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Sauté the diced onion until translucent, then stir in the tomato paste and cook for 2–3 minutes until it darkens.
  4. Combine and simmer
  5. Add the simmered pepper base to the pot. Stir in curry powder and thyme and cook for 2 minutes. Pour in the stock, add bay leaves, and bring to a boil. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Cook the rice
  7. Rinse the rice thoroughly under cold water until the water runs clear. Add the rinsed rice to the pot and stir. Reduce heat to low, cover tightly (using foil under the lid if needed for a good seal), and simmer for 25–30 minutes until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  8. Finish and serve

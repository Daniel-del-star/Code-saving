Story of the rabbit and the turtle

The rabbit was the fastest in the woods,but he was very cocky, so the turtle asked for a race but the turtle was very slow,because of that the rabbit knew the race was going to be easy, so the race started the rabbit was in the lead ,but he was so cocky that he went to sleep , he went to sleep for so long that the turtle was in the lead he tried to get there ,but it was too late
this teaches us to not be cocky

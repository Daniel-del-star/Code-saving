Lions are the only social cats, living in prides where lionesses do most of the hunting, and their roars can be heard up to 5 miles away. A male's mane signifies health and can be a deterrent during fights, while cubs are born helpless but start walking in about two weeks. Sadly, wild lion populations have declined drastically, with fewer than 24,000 remaining in the wild, and they face threats from poaching and habitat loss.
